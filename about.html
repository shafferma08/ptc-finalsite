About Pinellas Technical College

For over 60 years, PTC has been Pinellas County's trusted source for career and technical education, preparing students for high-demand jobs through hands-on, industry-certified training.

"Our mission is to provide students the opportunity to develop national workplace competencies to fill the needs of business and industry."

What We Aspire To

Our Vision

To be our communities' first choice for technical training.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

  • Focus on attracting and retaining students.
  • Implement and maintain systems to enable all students to succeed.
  • Deliver customized employer-driven training.
  • Be accountable for our students' learning outcomes.
  • Provide learning experiences when and where necessary to meet our customers' needs.
  • Maintain a safe environment conducive to learning.
  • Recruit and develop quality staff.

Our Story

Since 1962, Pinellas Technical College has offered students first-rate career and technical education at our two campuses in Clearwater and St. Petersburg, and at extension and clinical locations across the region.

PTC's emphasis is on postsecondary education focusing on over 40 career areas and about 60 programs, currently serving nearly 5,000 full-time students. We're proud of our network of more than 250 business and industry partners who advise and guide our programs to ensure they prepare students for the needs of the workforce.

PTC is also a leader in Florida in providing career and technical education via distance learning, supported by highly skilled faculty and staff who prepare graduates to compete in a global job market.

As part of Pinellas County Schools, PTC combines the resources of a public school system with the focused, career-driven mission of a technical college. Students benefit from affordable tuition, financial aid, and direct connections to local employers.

Two Campuses, One Mission

PTC's Clearwater and St. Petersburg campuses each offer a unique mix of industry-aligned programs, but both share the same commitment to preparing students for rewarding careers.

The Clearwater Campus sits at 6100 154th Ave N, and the St. Petersburg Campus sits at 901 34th St S. For the full list of programs offered at each campus, see the campus pages below.
