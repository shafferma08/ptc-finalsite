How Apprenticeships Work

Apprenticeships let you earn a wage while training for a skilled career. Here is what to expect.

1

Apply and Get Hired

You are employed by a sponsor company from day one. You earn a paycheck while attending classes at PTC.

2

Train On the Job

Split your time between hands-on work with your employer and classroom instruction at PTC-Clearwater.

3

Earn Your Credential

Complete the program with industry-recognized credentials, real experience, and zero student debt.
