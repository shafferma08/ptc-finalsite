About PTC Clearwater Campus

Find official documents for the Clearwater campus: catalog, accreditation, school improvement and safety reports, and how to request your records.

PTC's institutional history, mission, vision, and core values apply to both Clearwater and St. Petersburg campuses. To learn about PTC as a whole, visit About PTC.

The records on this page are specific to the Clearwater campus and are maintained per Council on Occupational Education (COE) accreditation requirements.
