Why Choose PTC

Hands-On Training. Real-World Skills.

For over 60 years, Pinellas Technical College has been preparing students for high-demand careers through immersive, hands-on training programs. Our graduates don't just earn certificates — they launch careers.

COE Accredited

Both campuses individually accredited by the Council on Occupational Education.

Industry Partnerships

Direct connections to Tampa Bay employers for internships and job placement.

Flexible Scheduling

Day, evening, and hybrid options so you can learn on your schedule.

Affordable Tuition

Financial aid, scholarships, and payment plans available to all students.
