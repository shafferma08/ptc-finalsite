About PTC St. Petersburg Campus

Find official documents for the St. Petersburg campus: catalog, accreditation, code of conduct, school improvement and safety reports, and how to request your records.

PTC's institutional history, mission, vision, and core values apply to both Clearwater and St. Petersburg campuses. To learn about PTC as a whole, visit About PTC.

The records on this page are specific to the St. Petersburg campus and are maintained per Council on Occupational Education (COE) accreditation requirements.

Most St. Petersburg programs meet at the main campus (901 34th St S). One section of Computer Systems & Information Technology meets at CVAEC (Clearview Adult Education Center), a separate PTC location. See the St. Petersburg class schedule for the location of each program.
