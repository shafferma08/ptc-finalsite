How to read this schedule

Days: M = Monday, T = Tuesday, W = Wednesday, R = Thursday, F = Friday.

Start Terms: Our academic year is divided into five terms. The numbers in this column show which terms you can start a program. For example, "1, 3, 5" means you can begin in Term 1, Term 3, or Term 5. Programs marked See program page follow a different enrollment timeline; visit the program page or contact a counselor for current start dates.

Main vs. CVAEC: Most programs meet at the main St. Petersburg campus (901 34th St S). One section of Computer Systems & Information Technology meets at CVAEC (Clearview Adult Education Center), a separate PTC location.

Program lengths and start dates are approximate and subject to change. Contact a counselor to confirm enrollment availability before applying.
