Request Your Student Records

Former PTC students can request copies of their class records and transcripts. Choose your campus below.

Content on this page is verbatim from www.myptc.edu — Request Your Student Records.

Records older than 5 years are maintained by the Central Records Department of Pinellas County Schools. Call 727-793-2701.

Step 1 — Download & complete the request form

Download the Request for School Records and Release Authorization Form. After completing the form, mail it to the PTC campus where you attended.

Step 2 — Mail or email to your campus
