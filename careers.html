Careers at PTC

Help shape the careers of adult learners in Pinellas County. PTC hires instructors, counselors, and support staff who care about hands-on, industry-aligned education.

How PTC hiring works: All PTC positions are posted through the Pinellas County Schools (PCSB) district careers system. PTC does not accept applications directly through this site. Select an open position in the district portal to apply.

Apply through Pinellas County Schools

PTC is part of Pinellas County Schools. Every instructional and support position is posted, reviewed, and filled through the PCSB careers system. Use the district portal to search for PTC openings by campus, category, or keyword.

Browse PCSB Careers

What to search for

  • "Pinellas Technical College"
  • "PTC Clearwater" or "PTC St. Petersburg"
  • Specific program (e.g., "Welding Instructor")
  • Category (Instructor, Counselor, Staff)

Types of Positions

PTC hires for three main categories of roles. Actual open postings change frequently and are the authoritative source of current opportunities.

Instructional

Program Instructors

Lead a full-time or short-course program. Industry experience and relevant certifications are typically required; teaching certification can often be earned on the job.

Instructional

Adult Education Instructors

Teach ABE, GED, ASE preparation, or ESOL to adult learners preparing for a high school equivalency diploma or improving basic skills.

Support

Counselors & Advisors

Guide students through program selection, enrollment, financial aid, and completion. Master's in counseling or related field typically required.

Support

Distance Learning & Technology

Support online course delivery, Canvas administration, and student-facing technology systems across both campuses.

Staff

Administrative & Operations

Registrar, financial aid, student records, communications, and facilities roles that keep the campus running.

Part-Time

Adjunct & Substitute Instructors

Teach evening or short-course sections on a part-time schedule. A good entry point for industry professionals new to teaching.

Hiring Process

Every PTC hire follows the PCSB district hiring workflow. Here's what to expect.

  1. Browse open positionsSearch the PCSB careers portal by keyword ("Pinellas Technical College" or a program name) and filter to the role type you want.
  2. Submit a district applicationComplete the PCSB application with work history, certifications, and references. Uploads typically include a résumé and relevant licenses.
  3. Screening and interviewsQualified candidates are invited to interview with the hiring manager and the PTC campus leadership team. Instructional roles may include a teaching demonstration.
  4. Background check and offerPCSB conducts fingerprinting and reference verification. Offers are extended through the district HR office.
  5. OnboardingPTC onboarding covers campus orientation, Canvas access, and instructional mentorship for new faculty.

Why Work at PTC

PTC employees receive Pinellas County Schools district benefits plus the opportunity to teach in an adult-focused, career-technical setting.

Health & Wellness

District-sponsored medical, dental, and vision coverage with options for employees and families.

Florida Retirement System

Participation in the FRS Pension or Investment plan, plus voluntary 403(b) and 457 options.

Paid Time Off

Sick leave, personal days, and paid holidays aligned with the PCSB school-year calendar.

Professional Development

Ongoing PLN courses, tuition reimbursement options, and industry certification support.

Adult Education Focus

Work with motivated adult learners in a hands-on, career-track environment — no K-12 classroom management.

Employer Partnerships

Connected to the local workforce through active advisory boards across every program category.

Questions About a Posting?

The PCSB HR office handles applicant questions, technical issues with the portal, and status updates. For questions specific to teaching at PTC or a particular program, contact the campus directly.
