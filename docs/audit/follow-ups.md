# Follow-Ups for PTC Live-Site Owners

This file collects items where our redesign audit surfaced gaps, asymmetries, or stale content on the **live PTC sites** (www.myptc.edu, clearwater.myptc.edu, stpete.myptc.edu) that need to be raised with the responsible page owners.

**Project rule:** Per Marianne's direction (2026-04-25), all redesign content must come from the approved live PTC sites verbatim. We never silently expand or augment in the redesign. Anything that should be improved gets logged here as a follow-up suggestion for PTC owners to act on, and we ship the live wording in the meantime.

---

## How to use this file

When auditing a cluster and finding live-site issues that aren't in our scope to fix:

1. Add a row to the appropriate section below
2. Include: live URL, the issue, the recommendation, who likely owns it (if known), priority, and the audit cluster + date that surfaced it
3. Marianne reviews periodically and routes items to the right owners (Webmaster role transition is July 2026)
