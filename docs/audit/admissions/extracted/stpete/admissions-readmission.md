# Readmission

Students must meet with a guidance counselor who will assist with
registration forms and literacy testing. (Test scores are valid for a
two-year period, provided there have been no changes in the literacy
levels mandated by state guidelines.) A meeting with the instructor may
be required before re-entering a program.

Note: Course credit toward program completion is valid for a period of three years from initial entry date.
