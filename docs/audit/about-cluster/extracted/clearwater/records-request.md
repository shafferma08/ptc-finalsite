(How to) Request Your Student Record(s) from PTC - Clearwater

Request for School Records

To request a copy of your class records, download the form that must be completed.  After you have completed the Request for School Records and Release Authorization form mail it to the PTC campus where you attended.

Pinellas Technical College - Clearwater
Attn: Records Department
6100 154th Ave North
Clearwater, FL 33760

Clearwater: Request For School Records and Release Authorization Form

Email to canfieldj@pcsb.org

Note:  Program records more than 5 years old are maintained by the Central Records Department of Pinellas County Schools; they can be reached at 727-793-2701.
