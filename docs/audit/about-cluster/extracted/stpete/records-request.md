(How to) Request Your Student Record(s) from PTC - St. Petersburg

How to Request Your Student Record
Request for School Records

To request a copy of your class records, Download Form that must be completed.  After you have completed the Request for School Records and Release Authorization form mail it to the PTC campus you attended (in this case, St. Petersburg).

Pinellas Technical College - St. Petersburg
Attn: Records Department
901 34th Street South
St. Petersburg, FL 33711
e-mail to kilpatrickc@pcsb.org

Note:  Program records more than 5 years old are maintained by the Central Records Department of Pinellas County Schools; they can be reached at 727-793-2701.
