Get to Know PTC!

Welcome to

Since 1962, our institution has offered students first-rate career and technical education at our two campuses – Clearwater and St. Petersburg – and at extension and clinical locations.  Pinellas Technical College's emphasis is on postsecondary education focusing on over 40 exciting career areas and about 60 programs and currently has nearly 5,000 full-time students enrolled.  Pinellas Technical College also offers continuing education courses and commercial training services through our Industry Services Department.

Your Pinellas Technical College experience will be rich with relevant, stimulating, and challenging career and technical education.  We are proud of our network of more than 250 business and industry partners who advise and guide our programs to ensure they are preparing students for the needs of the work force.
