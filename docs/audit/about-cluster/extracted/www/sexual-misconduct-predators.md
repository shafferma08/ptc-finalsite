Sexual Misconduct

Pinellas Technical College is committed to providing an environment which is free from all forms of sexual misconduct including sexual harassment, public indecency, voyeurism, battery, or assault. Anyone accused of such charges may be subject to dismissal from the college. Victims must contact the director of their campus (Clearwater - 727.538.7167 or St. Petersburg - 727.893.2500), and they will be provided guidance, assistance and support.
