Visitor Information

A few things to know before you arrive at either campus.

Check-In

All visitors sign in at the main office and receive a visitor badge. Please allow a few extra minutes on your first visit.

Parking

Parking is free for visitors. Avoid spaces marked "Reserved" or "Staff Only." Accessible spaces are near each entrance.

Public Transit

Both campuses are served by PSTA bus routes. Plan your trip at psta.net.

Campus Safety

PTC campuses follow district-wide PCSB safety procedures. In an emergency, dial 911 and then contact the main office.

Schedule a Campus Tour

The best way to get a feel for PTC is to see it in person. Tours let you walk the labs, meet instructors, and ask questions before you apply.
