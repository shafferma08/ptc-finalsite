Post a Job Opportunity

FREE FOR EMPLOYERS

There is no fee to post your job opening at Pinellas Technical College. Your position will be listed on Career Rocket, PTC's job board built exclusively for our students and graduates. It could be the gateway to your next great hire!

How It Works

1

Contact Us

Email our Placement Coordinator to get started with your free job posting.

2

We Set You Up

We'll create your employer account on Career Rocket so you can manage your listings.

3

Start Hiring

Post positions, job descriptions, skills required, salary info, and connect with PTC talent.

Ready to Post Your Opening?

Contact our Placement Coordinator to get your free employer account set up on Career Rocket.
